Procedure for Hardness of Water Experiment

  1. Prepare the water sample to be tested.
  2. Add a few drops of Eriochrome Black T (EBT) indicator to the water sample.
  3. Fill the burette with EDTA solution.
  4. Slowly add EDTA from the burette to the water sample while stirring.
  5. Observe the color change from wine red to blue at the endpoint.
  6. Record the volume of EDTA used at the endpoint.
  7. Calculate the hardness of water using the formula:
    • Hardness (ppm) = Volume of EDTA (mL) × 10
